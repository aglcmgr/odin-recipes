Sun-Dried Tomato Pasta with Italian Sausage

⏱️ 30 mins ⭐ Easy 👥 4 servings

This Sun-Dried Tomato Pasta with Italian Sausage is a flavorful and hearty dish that combines the rich taste of sun-dried tomatoes with the savory goodness of Italian sausage. Perfect for a quick weeknight meal or a cozy weekend dinner, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings for comfort food with a twist.

Image credits to Lorie Yarro ~ Lemons&Zest

The combination of sun-dried tomatoes, garlic, and Italian sausage creates a deliciously robust sauce that coats the pasta perfectly. Whether you're a fan of spicy or mild flavors, you can adjust the level of heat by choosing your preferred type of Italian sausage. This dish is not only easy to make but also packed with flavor, making it a great choice for any pasta lover.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. pasta (penne, rigatoni, or your choice)
  • 1 lb. Italian sausage (mild or spicy, casings removed)
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes (packed in oil, drained and chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Equipments Needed

  1. ✓ Large pot for boiling pasta
  2. ✓ Large skillet or frying pan
  3. ✓ Colander for draining pasta
  4. ✓ Wooden spoon or spatula for stirring
  5. ✓ Measuring cups and spoons

Image credits to Lorie Yarro ~ Lemons&Zest

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the Italian sausage over medium heat until browned and cooked through. Break it up into small pieces as it cooks. Remove the sausage from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and chopped sun-dried tomatoes. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Return the cooked sausage to the skillet and stir to combine with the garlic and sun-dried tomatoes.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Let it cook for 3-4 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat it evenly with the sauce.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired, and serve hot.
