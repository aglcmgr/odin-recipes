Creamy Beef and Mushroom

⏱️ 5 mins ⭐ Easy 👥 4 servings

Creamy Beef with Mushroom is a delicious Filipino dish featuring tender beef sirloin cooked in a rich, creamy mushroom sauce. The beef is slowly simmered until it becomes fork-tender, then combined with button mushrooms and a creamy Knorr mushroom soup base for a hearty, satisfying meal.

Image credits to Panlasang Pinoy | Vanjo Merano

The dish combines the savory, umami flavors of mushrooms with tender, juicy beef. The garlic and onion add depth, while the creamy sauce brings everything together into one mouthwatering dish. It's comfort food at its best! Best served over warm steamed rice to soak up all the delicious creamy sauce. Perfect for family dinners or when you want to impress guests without spending hours in the kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. beef sirloin sliced thinly
  • 8 ounces button mushrooms
  • 62 grams Knorr Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 head garlic sliced
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon parsley chopped
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Equipments Needed

  1. ✓ Large pan or skillet with lid
  2. ✓ Cutting board
  3. ✓ Sharp knife
  4. ✓ Measuring cups and spoons
  5. ✓ Wooden spoon or spatula (for stirring)
  6. ✓ Bowl (for mixing Knorr sauce)
  7. ✓ Serving plate or bowls

Image credits to Panlasang Pinoy | Vanjo Merano

Cooking Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a pan. Add cooking oil.
  2. Sauté garlic until it browns. Add onion and cook until it softens.
  3. Add the beef. Cook while stirring until the sides turn brown.
  4. Pour 1 cup water. Cover the pan. Adjust to a simmer and continue cooking for 35 minutes.
  5. Add button mushrooms. Stir.
  6. Combine 1 cup water and Knorr Cream of Mushroom in a bowl. Mix well.
  7. Pour the mixture into the pan. Let it boil. Continue cooking uncovered for 10 to 15 minutes using low heat setting.
  8. Season with salt and ground black pepper.
  9. Top with parsley. Serve warm with rice.
