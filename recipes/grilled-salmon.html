Grilled Salmon

⏱️ 10 mins ⭐ Easy 👥 6 servings

This Grilled Salmon is a simple and delicious way to prepare fresh salmon. Perfect for a healthy meal, this recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings for flavorful and nutritious food.

Image credits to Allrecipes | Julia Hartbeck

It is fresh salmon cooked on a hot grill until it develops beautiful char marks and reaches the perfect internal temperature. Grilling is a quick cooking method that adds delicious smoky flavor to the fish. The main challenge is that salmon's high moisture content can make it stick to the grill, so careful attention and proper technique are essential for perfect results every time.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds salmon fillets
  • Lemon pepper to taste
  • Garlic powder to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • ⅓ cup soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup water
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil

Equipments Needed

  1. ✓ Grill (gas, charcoal, or wood-burning)
  2. ✓ Grill grates or grill mat
  3. ✓ Tongs or long-handled fork
  4. ✓ Fish spatula (or regular spatula)
  5. ✓ Grill brush (for cleaning grates)
  6. ✓ Meat thermometer
  7. ✓ Cutting board
  8. ✓ Sharp knife
  9. ✓ Bowl (for marinade/glaze)
  10. ✓ Measuring cups and spoons
  11. ✓ Heat-resistant gloves (optional)

Image credits to Allrecipes | Julia Hartbeck

Cooking Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.
  3. Stir soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil together in a small bowl until sugar is dissolved. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag; add soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  5. Place salmon on the preheated grill, and discard marinade. Cook salmon until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 6 to 8 minutes per side.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
